Vanishing Emails

When something loses its purpose,
it should disappear.

Digital waste is invisible because it has no physical form. A promotional email that has expired is not just useless, it is waste with a timestamp. We gave it a natural end. What felt like a sustainability insight was actually a product logic waiting to be built.
